# 🧾 1. BRD (Business Requirements Document)

## 🎯 Product Vision
A **College OS** where students access notes, interact, review professors/colleges, and build academic identity.

## 👥 Target Users
*   **Students** (Primary consumers)
*   **Contributors** (Seniors/Toppers who upload notes)
*   **Recruiters** (Future B2B users for talent acquisition)
*   **Admins / Moderators** (Ensuring content quality)

## 💡 Core Value
*   **Structured Notes:** Organized by College → Course → Semester → Subject (Not chaos).
*   **Real Student Interaction:** Peer-to-peer discussions and doubt solving.
*   **Trust via Ratings:** Community-driven quality control for notes and professors.
*   **Academic Identity:** Future roadmap includes Academic Rating System (ARS).

## 💰 Revenue Strategy (Phase-wise)
### Phase 1:
*   **Google Ads:** Base monetization via traffic.
*   **Affiliate:** Referral links for academic courses and books.

### Phase 2:
*   **Featured Notes:** Promoted content for creators.
*   **Sponsorships:** College-level events or brand promotions.

### Phase 3:
*   **Recruiter Access:** B2B subscription for accessing top-performing students based on academic identity.
